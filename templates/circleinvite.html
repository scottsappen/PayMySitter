Circle Invites

The circle concept

The idea of the circle is easy. Your circle is a hand-picked group of babysitters and families that you trust with your childcare.

If you are a sitter, your circle contains the families that you babysit for.

Whether the family invites you to their circle, or you request to be in a family's circle, this feature allows you to manage your jobs and contacts. To make payment seamless, don't forget to complete your banking information inside of your profile.

If you are a parent, your circle is made up of your favorite babysitters and other families that you may share babysitters with.

Inviting your babysitters to your circle is easy! Just send them an invitation and the sitter will receive a notification to join PayMySitter. After the sitter is in your circle, you can book and pay them right from the app. Need a recommendation for a babysitter? Connect with other families to view their sitters and connect with them from their profiles. Don't want to share? No problem! You can keep your babysitters all to yourself by not connecting with other families.
